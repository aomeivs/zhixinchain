FAQ
  • What is Bright Bitcoin(BBTC)?
    Bright Bitcoin (BBTC) is a fork of Bitcoin that occurs at the predetermined height of block 506,666.The fork will make improvements of the Bitcoin community with Real-name authentication mechanism, and bright the Bitcoin accounts for maintaining a decentralized safety network on the premise of complete confidentiality of payment.
  • What is the difference between the Bright Bitcoin and the Bitcoin?
    Compared with the cryptonym of Bitcoin , BBTC is Real-name authenticated. Any account and transaction in BBTC can trace back to the true person. In conclusion, BBTC has the features of keeping blockchain away from money laundering、Darknet and illegal trade.
  • What is the total amount of Bright Bitcoin(BBTC)? Will it be pre-mined?
    The total amount of BBTC is 21,210,000, of which 210,000 pre-mined tokens will be managed by the maintenance of BBTC, invested in the BBTC ecosystem and the market expansion.
  • How can I get Bright Bitcoin?
    Anyone who holds Bitcoin at the predetermined height of block 506,666 will become owners of Bright Bitcoin, which is 1 BTC: 1 BBTC. For example, if you have 10 Bitcoin, then you will own 10 BBTC after forked. In addition, you can also get BBTC by mining or exchanging it at any related markets.
