What is ZhixinChain?

ZhixinChain is a professional credit platform based on the block chain technology.
It is an objective recorder for a person's workplace behavior.
Records such as post, labor contract, salary situation, and monthly attendance, including four aspects of value orientation:

ZhixinChain can solve enterprise management by recording the workplace monthly for employees Credit assets.

In the ZhixinChain, the employees' career record information, such as employee post information, job performance, reward and punishment record and so on, will be recorded by the enterprise. When employees change their work units, the information will follow the staff to the next work unit. Because of the nature of the underlying technology of the block chain, it is determined that the information can never be tampered with.

Through the ZhixinChain, the enterprises can fully and accurately understand the employees' work experience and so on.

For employees, ZhixinChain is a reliable personal digital asset platform to help employees accumulate valuable credit digital assets scattered and lost, and ZhixinChain is the personal professional credit digital asset platform for the future.

ZhixinChain is a virtue book. The hard work of the staff is to accumulate credit, accumulate for tomorrow, and lay a good foundation for the future. The ZhixinChain is the best voucher for his future value.

FAQ

  • Q
    How do you deal with personal privacy?
    A
    ① when employees are in service, they can open their Zhixin to their colleagues.
    ② At any time,you must have acquired the person’s admission first,which allows you to browse his Zhixin,if you are not his current colleague .
    ③ Any department can search for someone by his name to know if he has a Zhixin record,but you cannot browse the details without his permission.
  • Q
    How do you deal with the Commercial Confidential Information of your Zhixin company users？
    A
    ① To those excellent employees ,when they are in service,any one from outside the company can not browse his Zhixin details even with his permission.
    ② To those non-excellent employees,when they are in service,any one from outside the company can browse his Zhixin details with his permission.
    ③ Company users can choose to describe employees’s working conditions in a relative way,instead of a absolute way,such as ‘among top rankings within month’,rather than’ 50,000 yuan as performance’.
  • Q
    How do you release users’ Zhixin onto the platform？
    A
    ① Users’ company authorized(can be graded) their HR or any one related to release.
    ② The Zhixin content is divided into two major categories of objective and subjective.Any information released on the platform, the employee himself can post one of the following 3 opinions as ‘agree’,’disagree’,’no comments(comments shall be made with one month)’on the platform,and can post personal comments on certain information.
    ③ When company users activated the Zhixin system,they can re-enter their employees Zhixin information from up to 6 months ago.
  • Q
    Can I change or delete informations that have been released？
    A
    ① No,you can’t.However its status can be set from open to hidden.
    ② Zhixin is based on block chain,therefor ,once it has been released ,it can never be changed or deleted,that’s why Zhixin is reliable.
    ③ If some error informations have been release about any employees, the informations can then be appended to certain month.
